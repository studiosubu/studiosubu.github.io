Saffron White & Green

T-shirt Collection



Brief

Saffron White And Green (SWAG) is a t-shirt manufacturing firm that specializes is design collections inspired by cities of India. SWAG was developing their upcoming Pondicherry collection and was looking for very fresh, pure designs that stayed true to the coastal city. We were provided with a deck of photographs showcasing architecture, nature, and lifestyle of the site.

Solution

We created a series of over 50 designs ranging from rotary prints, to abstract crops, pocket prints, and aztec designs. The designs had a dynamic color palette of bright neons juxtaposed with muted earthy tones. Each individual design had elements rooted to the photographs they were inspired by.

Impact

The prints are still in production. The collection is scheduled to launch in the latter half of this year.
