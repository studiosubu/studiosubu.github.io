Redrawing India

book cover


Brief

In celebrating Teach For Indiaâ€™s fifth year of operations, Founder & CEO Shaheen Mistri wrote a book reflecting on the first 5 years of the organization. The book was a unique and important communication channel through which we hoped to reach a wide audience of readers to share our work and vision for an excellent education for all children.



Solution

The cover of the book needed to represent the truth and hope of the country and its children. We used a poignant, yet joyous photograph to emote our story, and the Teach For India logo colors painted on the title of the book to emphasize our mission.



Impact

Over 3000 copies of the book were sold in the first 3 months, and it is available on all major online e-commerce portals in India and abroad.
