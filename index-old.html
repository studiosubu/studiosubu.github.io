Subu is a Mumbai-based independent design studio run by Harish Subramanian.
The studio specializes in building branding systems, and providing high quality design and product solutions on print and online platforms. It doesn't matter how small or massive your project may be, as long as you have a great concept, good taste and some money, the studio will surprise you with the most carefully crafted, sustainable creative solution.
Subu brings a wide array of creative collaborators across the country together ranging from
graphic designers, to developers, print shops, interaction designers, product designers,
photographers, videographers, and a variety of artists.



Harish Subramanian
Founder / Graphic Designer
Harish Subramanian
Founder / Graphic Designer

Harish Subramanian has worked as a visual designer for several years with non-profits and companies like Teach For India, and JWT New York. His work has also been internationally recognized and awarded the William Drenttel Award for Excellence in Design. Harish holds a BFA degree from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, and has collaborated with designers and creatives across different genres. He has been trained in diverse fields of study, like print production, film & video production, marketing & HR theories, ethnographic research techniques, and human sensorial studies in anthropology. When not working, Harish is either watching rom-coms, making doodles, or sitting at a quiet bar.

Gagan Minhas
Visual designer
Gagan Minhas
Visual designer

Gagan Minhas has previously worked at Beard Design & Foxymoron and is now working as a Freelance Graphic designer. He was at Symbiosis Institute of Design for two and a half years until he decided to call it quits. He is fascinated with designing for the digital world, and building brands from ground zero. When not designing he’s usually out on the streets longboarding or spending time on his terrace watching the sunset.

Meghna Rakshit
Communications strategist
Meghna Rakshit
Communications strategist

Meghna has over 10 years of marketing and advertising experience in NYC and Mumbai. She is currently the Communications Director at Teach For India. Prior to TFI, she has designed 360 marketing solutions for brands like Unilever, Idea Cellular and Kelloggs through her work at Mindshare. Meghna has an MBA degree from the Indian School of Business and a BA degree in communications and psychology from Hamilton College, NY. Aside from work, she is a strong believer in social change, obsessed with music, inspired by people, and balanced by Buddhism.

Prashin Jagger
Photographer
Prashin Jagger
Photographer

Prashin Jagger believes in providing what's true to the moment. Coming from an engineering background, he has a varied experience of 3 years in photography. Prashin brings a mix of a finesse, rawness and emotion to his work. He has worked with music magazines like Rolling Stone India, and Rock Street Journal, and has been associated with brands like Rayban, Jack Daniels, Puma, Youtube, OML and Sula Wines. Prashin has also photographed weddings across the country. When not working, he's either watching TV series, working at a farm or making music.

Rhimjim Surana
Researcher / Interaction designer

Suraj Kalra
Photographer
Suraj Kalra
Visual designer

Having lived in Bombay, Singapore, UK (Nottingham) and Hong Kong, Suraj has worked across cultures performing project management and handling communications for a range of events such as award shows, art exhibitions, and gala dinners. Being exposed to several cultures, Suraj began to question his own identity, and picked up his camera as a means to explore and express himself. He finds inspiration in the streets and likes to challenge commonly held biases through his lens. His pictures have been featured in CNN, Fox News, South China Morning Post Magazine, Daily Mail and other blogs. In his free time he's either wandering or trying to avoid malls after having hit his head onto glass windows one time too many.

Astha Govil
Brand consultant
Astha Govil
Brand consultant

Astha Govil is a creative problem solver, brand manager/consultant and a fashion designer. She specialises in Marketing and Situational Analysis- identifying opportunities and creating strategies for new businesses, start ups and expansion of existing brand names. Astha holds a Master's in Fashion Management from Domus Academy in Milan. She has worked on strategy building projects for the likes of Salvatore Ferragamo, Moncler and various other luxury brands. When free, Astha enjoys strange intellectual conversations with stranger people.
