Treelife Consulting

Branding



Brief

Treelife is a small start-up providing accounting, taxation and regulatory services. It was looking to build an identity for basic stationery and social media advertising. Treelife wanted to set a new tone in the small-scale consulting market.

Solution

We built a gentle and simplistic identity for Treelife. The tree branch expresses protection, shelter, and subtly hints at a ‘helping hand’. The color chosen has a fresh, relaxing, earthy tone. We incorporated a stylized version of the rings found on a sliced bark, as a symbol for growth, strength and progress. We hand-picked a light, recycled paper for the pocket sized brochures, to keep the brand elements blended with its name. We also re-worked all content and copy of the brand in order to preserve its inviting, non-corporate tone. View website at www.treelifeconsulting.in

Impact

Treelife has built a strong client base of over 15 entities in a short span of 3 months. It has recently tied up with a company with an annual revenue of 100+ crores.
