Teach for India Staff Book

Book Design



Brief

Teach For India was looking to make a compact brand book to hand over to all staff. Until now, they hadnâ€™t developed a strong brand guideline to work off, and hence gave us a blank canvas, a photo archive, and an extremely tight budget to work with.

Solution

We designed an extremely minimal, delicate little book where we made our photos the hero. The text complimented the photographs, and the content in the photos reflected what you read on every page. We introduced very silent design elements, to guide the reader and build a flow. Developing a photo-heavy book helped make the content feel lighter and more dynamic to read.
