Subu is a creative collective based in Mumbai. The studio specializes in building core branding systems, and providing high quality creative solutions in print and online platforms. It doesn’t matter how little or massive your project may be, as far as you have a great concept, good taste and some money, the studio will be game to surprise you. Subu collaborates with designers, coders, print shops, photographers, videographers and innovative thinkers across the city to provide you with smart, sustainable creative solutions.

Our process involves a deep dive into design+ethnographic+market study of your brand, followed by research-centric concept development. We stay heavily invested in the production process to preserve quality and consistency at every stage of execution. Our creatives are built with care, craft and precision.






FOUNDER

Harish Subramanian has worked as a designer for several years with non-profits and companies like Teach For India, and JWT New York. Subu is an initiative to introduce high quality, delicately crafted, personalized branding solutions to the Indian market. Harish holds a BFA degree from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, and has collaborated with designers and creatives across different genres. He has been trained in diverse fields of study, like print production, film & video production, marketing & HR theories, ethnographic research techniques, and human sensorial studies in anthropology. When not working, Harish is either watching rom-coms, making art, or sitting at a quiet bar.
