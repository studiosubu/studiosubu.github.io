Future School of Performing Arts

Branding



Brief

FSPA is a non-profit performing arts school & studio space. The organization was looking to build a simple, grounded brand identity, with an extensible branding system to support its varied scope of creative collaterals.

Solution

The identity embodies the angles, lines, form, and structure seen in dance. It emulates that one sharp move that takes a routine to the next level – the edge of the future. It is a creative representation of FSPA’s vision of empowering individuals towards excellence. We’ve helped build a comprehensive communication system in the form of a brand manual that not only reflects the mood and personality of the organization, but also exhibits the ability to be a self-sustaining, scalable framework. The manual provides a detailed set of usage guidelines for all creative executions including photography, layouts, brand-level promotions.
