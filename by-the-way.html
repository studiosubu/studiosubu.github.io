By The Way - The Parsi Kitchen

Rebranding, Exterior design



Brief

By The Way, is an old Parsi restaurant owned and run by a well-established non-profit organisation - Seva Sadan Society. The restaurant hadn’t ever invested in a streamlined branding as such, and hence was looking for fresh identity that could hopefully better its sales, performance and impact. Having majority of its clientele in the late 40’s category, By The Way was also looking to extend its reach to the untapped youth market.

Solution

The identity exhibits a balance between a mature and a youthful vibe. The typeface stays true to the Parsi flavor and personality. We worked with colors existing in the restaurant interiors to sync all visual elements. Post building its identity, we went further to help rebuild their external façade to streamline with our proposed creative. We stayed heavily invested in the implementation process — from repainting the walls to producing the signage and light box. The studio is currently reworking the restaurant’s print collaterals.
