Teach For India National Recruitment Campaign 2014-15

Campaign



Brief

Teach For India considers its annual recruitment campaign as one of its largest, most crucial creative projects of the year. The organization is heavily dependent on it to drive traffic to the website and thereby help recruit applicants for the Fellowship Program. This year, Teach For India decided to change the recruitment tag line to “find your light” and was hoping to develop an effective end-to-end print + digital campaign.

Solution

We developed a Fellow-focused call-to-action, where the “find your light” phrase had a missing “i”. The missing “i” stood for the search for your own potential, your own light. You could find that light if you took on the challenge to teach, and help the kids discover their potential. This indirect style of messaging was translated on to multiple elements in the campaign. We created a series of thought-provoking word-play print/ digital posters, and balanced it out with an emotive, photo-centric “Light is” series. We streamlined all the creatives with consistent typography, a bold yellow-black color palette, and minimal design elements.

Impact

Teach For India received over 14,000 applications and claimed to have an 8% selectivity rate, which is by far the highest the organization has every had since its beginning. The theme has now been confirmed to be re-used in the forthcoming 2015 Annual Recruitment Campaign.
