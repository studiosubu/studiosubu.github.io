All services offered have one key element in common — The Design Process.
Every project begins with a research study of your initiative’s structure, model, growth plan, and participating stakeholders. This is followed by a concept-driven approach in developing design solutions. The studio strives to find answers that are backed by strong reasoning and powerful stories. It stays heavily invested in the production process to preserve quality and consistency at every stage of execution, be it print, digital or spacial. The studio takes pride in handing over simple, smart, sustainable solutions. Subu’s creatives are built with care, craft, and precision.




Branding
system

Your identity would be a summation of your logo, the color palette, the typography, and the design elements that weave together to build your brand. The way you use these elements to communicate will determine how effective your brand recall value is. The core branding system contains your identity and a comprehensive study of usage methods and guidelines customized for your brand’s requirements. The studio specializes in building such concept-drive systems that are flexible, scalable, and sustainable.

Product
solutions

Subu helps you build scalable, well-designed products for your business. From web and mobile applications, to designing collections and series for varied surfaces and platforms, the studio builds them all. Subu invests time in living and breathing your brand, before developing your product. It works in sync with your brand targets and goals. It collaborates with a wide array of versatile artists and creative thinkers to bring you the most customized product. Your product goes through a range of processes (qualitative research, story boards, mood boards, mind-maps, UI/UX strategy, user testing) to preserve its authenticity, and reflect your brand's vision.
